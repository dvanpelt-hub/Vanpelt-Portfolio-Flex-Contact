About Me

I am a student at SNHU pursuing a B.S. in Finance (expected completion: 07/2021). For the past nine years I have served and worked with the US military, both overseas and within the US. This has resulted in a dynamic lifestyle which keeps me traveling frequently. I spend part of my time in London and the rest in Florida. Although I loved traveling and my previous work, I have recently decided it was time for a career change. Web and Software Development has been an interest of mine for quite some time, and I'm now making my transition into the industry!

I've chosen to participate in the Web Developer bootcamp offered through Bloc and feel excited to meet others within the industry. Challenges and problem solving draw me in, and working with a team dedicated to completing projects and solving problems is exactly what I'm looking for. I am able to collaborate and work independently on web development, using HTML, CSS, and JS. Additionally, as my skills progress I will update my list of services offered via this portfolio. If you are looking for someone to assist in projects, please feel free to contact me!
